  • Interactive org chart and reporting lines

Attendance & Workflow Management

Whether your team works from the office, home, or the field — capture every clock-in accurately and surface real-time data for managers and HR alike.

Every organisation has unique leave requirements. Staafin's flexible policy builder gives you full control without complex configuration.

Asset Management

Stop losing track of laptops, phones, and equipment. Staafin brings full asset lifecycle management into your HR platform.

Hiring & Recruitment

From your first job post to a signed offer, Staafin's ATS keeps every stakeholder aligned and candidates moving through the pipeline.
