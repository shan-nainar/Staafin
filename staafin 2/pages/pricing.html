Frequently asked questions

Is there really no credit card required for the free trial?

Correct — no credit card, no commitment. You get 14 days of full access to all features. Upgrade only when you're ready.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How is the per-user pricing calculated?

You're billed for active employees in your account each month. Archived employees don't count toward your bill.

Is Staafin GDPR compliant?

Yes. Staafin is fully GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we provide data processing agreements for all paid plans.
